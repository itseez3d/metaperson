Discover the potential of MetaPerson - [Unity SALSA LipSync Sample](https://github.com/avatarsdk/metaperson-salsa-sample), a comprehensive demonstration of integrating MetaPerson avatars into Unity using the [SALSA LipSync Suite](https://assetstore.unity.com/packages/tools/animation/salsa-lipsync-suite-148442) package. Designed for developers working with Unity 2021.3.19f1 or newer, this sample project showcases how to animate avatars with real-time lipsync, providing a robust foundation for creating more immersive and interactive experiences. 

With easy setup through the OneClick Base package, you can quickly configure your avatars for seamless facial animations. Whether you're building games, virtual worlds, or interactive media, this project equips you with the tools to bring your digital characters to life.
