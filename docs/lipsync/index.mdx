# Metaperson and Lipsync

MetaPerson avatars are designed to seamlessly integrate with leading lipsync technologies, enabling realistic and expressive speech synchronization for your projects. Whether you're working in Unity, Unreal Engine, or another platform, MetaPerson provides the flexibility to enhance your avatars with high-quality lipsync capabilities. For detailed implementation, refer to the sections below.

MetaPerson avatars support a comprehensive viseme set, 'visemes_15', which includes a total of 14 visemes and 1 additional blendshape for Laughter. This set is identical to the Oculus Lipsync visemes, making it straightforward to integrate with popular lipsync tools.

Here is the complete list of supported visemes:

- PP
- FF
- TH
- DD
- kk
- CH
- SS
- nn
- RR
- aa
- E
- ih
- oh
- ou
- Laughter (blendshape)

For more details, refer to the official [Oculus Lipsync documentation](https://developers.meta.com/horizon/documentation/unity/audio-ovrlipsync-viseme-reference).
